This research explores whether machine learning can predict the experimental conditions, solvent ratio, column type, temperature, that bring a polymer to its critical point in liquid chromatography (LCCC).

I am collaborating with Yongmei Wang, Professor of Chemistry at the University of Memphis, whose paper, [PolyCrit: An Online Collaborative Platform for Polymer Characterization](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.chroma.2025.465821), introduced a curated database of 428 confirmed critical conditions collected from over 300 papers. This work uses that dataset as training data for a predictive model.

The goal is a model that can suggest likely experimental conditions for new polymer and column combinations, reducing the trial-and-error currently required to find them by hand.

This site is still being developed; expect this page and the rest of the blog to continue updating as the research progresses.
